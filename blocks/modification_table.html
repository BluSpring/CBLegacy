Change those patterns!

So you want to modify your patterns?

Well that is amazing! Simply put the pattern in the left hand slot of the modification table, and select the modification you want.

The output pattern will appear on the right hand side.

You will see a preview in the lower area, click and drag to rotate, or scroll to zoom in and out.
