The idea of chiseling fluids not make any sense too you?

Well look no further than the bit storage, allowing the conversion between fluid and bits via a convenient tank.

You can convert any fluid too and from its bit form using it.

Can I store full blocks in it?

Yes. You can store normal blocks inside of it. And you will need to when used with a printer...
