Have you make a particular block you really want more of?

Then you can make a simple pattern by writing down the bits you used in a block.

How do you make it?

Use a quill together with a piece of paper in the other hand to write down the bits of the chiseled block you are looking at.

How do I modify it?

Put it in a modification table to modify it.

You can do modifications like:

  • Rotate
  • Mirror

How often can I use it?

Once. You will need to seal it to use it multiple times.
