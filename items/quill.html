--- layout: item category: items permalink: items/quill/ item: quill title: Quill ---

Writes down the chiseling pattern of chiseled blocks (sometimes works on unchiseled blocks) and allows you as such to create patterns for reuse.

When you are looking at a chiseled block, with a piece of paper in the other hand, and right click, then the writing will start, it is complete when the animation finishes.
