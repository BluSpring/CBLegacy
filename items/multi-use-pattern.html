These single use patterns keep on breaking! How do I make them last?

You can apply a sealant to a single use pattern to seal the pattern, which will allow you to use it multiple times.

Hold the sealant in one hand and then apply it to the pattern in your other hand.

Can I then still modify it?

No, you sealed your pattern. Writing on sealant seems to be impossible...
